This Privacy Policy describes how ToneSlice ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the ToneSlice mobile application (Bundle ID: com.toneslice.ToneSlice) on iOS devices.

1. Introduction

ToneSlice is a ringtone creation tool that lets you import, trim, export, and set custom ringtones from audio and video sources on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • When you send feedback via email, we receive the content of your message and your email address.

Information processed automatically:

  • Subscription and purchase status is processed through Apple and RevenueCat to verify your Pro entitlement. We do not collect or store payment card details.
  • Basic device information (such as device type, iOS version, and app version) may be transmitted to Apple or RevenueCat as part of standard in-app purchase processing.

Information we do not collect:

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not upload your audio files, ringtones, or recordings to our servers.
  • We do not currently use analytics or advertising SDKs that track you across apps.

3. How We Use Information

We use the limited information described above to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's core features
  • Process and verify subscriptions
  • Respond to your feedback and support requests
  • Improve the app based on user communications

4. Data Storage

Your ringtones, imported audio, and editing projects are stored locally on your device. App preferences (such as language selection and temporary ad-unlock expiry) are stored in your device's local storage (UserDefaults).

When you uninstall the app, locally stored data is removed from your device. We do not maintain copies of your content on our servers.

5. Device Permissions

ToneSlice may request the following permissions to provide its features:

  • Photo Library – to import audio and video from your photo library for ringtone creation.
  • Microphone – to record custom audio for use as ringtones.
  • Apple Music / Media Library – to import songs from your music library as ringtone sources.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in iOS Settings. Revoking a permission may limit related app features.

6. Third-Party Services

ToneSlice uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple – App Store purchases, subscription billing, and Apple Music access. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat – subscription management and entitlement verification. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

7. Children's Privacy

ToneSlice is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete information we hold about you.

  • To delete locally stored app data, uninstall ToneSlice from your device.
  • To manage or cancel subscriptions, use your Apple ID subscription settings in iOS Settings or the App Store.
  • To request information about data we may hold from your feedback emails, contact us at the address below.

9. Data Security

Because your audio content stays on your device, the primary security measure is your device's own protections (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). We take reasonable steps to protect any information you send us via email.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
